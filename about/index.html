I love to make things!

After training in Surface Design and working as commercial textile designer, I currently live in New York City and spend as much time as possible drawing, stitching, and making.

Pattern, texture, line, and shape are the focus of my hand-made work. I’m inspired by many things including folk-art, crafts, textiles, sculpture, and the Japanese aesthetic of wabi-sabi.
